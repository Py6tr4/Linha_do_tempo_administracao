Olá nós somos a Studium

A Studium

Will be an APP with the main objective of helping all educational institutions. It will help from the youngest to the oldest, allowing teachers to share their content in an easier, faster and, above all, fun and attractive way for students. The application will provide templates for creative activities, facilitating the work of teachers and the study of students in an organized and optimized way. In addition, institutions that adopt Studium will be able to manage grades, send reminders and publicize events 100% online, eliminating the need for printed posters and contributing to the preservation of the environment. An important difference is that the application will be completely free, guaranteeing access to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. The only requirements to use it are to have a mobile device and internet access.
